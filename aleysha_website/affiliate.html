So you've got the basics down, have your overlays and a few followers, but now you want to start earning some profit from streaming and not just hope on getting a few donations. The path to affliate is a hard one to get started but a rewarding one to achieve. Networking with other streamers is the main thing that will help you reach it but Twitch also have some achievements you have to complete before you can be an affliate.
How do I get there?
New day, new stream
Stream for 8 hours in the last 30 days. As simple as that.
Like clockwork
Stream on different days to reach different viewers. Unique days are based on GMT.
Who watches the watchers?
Reach 3 average viewers in the last 30 days. Keep an eye on your stats page for your adverage viewers.
What is thy bidding?
Reach 50 followers. Networking will help you reach your follower goals! Lurk in others small streamers streams and they might come to yours.
