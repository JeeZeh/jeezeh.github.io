Path to Partner
You reached affliate, you've decided you quite like this streaming thing and you want to keep at it. Now the hard work really begins. Although the goals for getting to partner are similar to that of affliate, you have to get a lot more viewers, stream for a longer time and for more days. Getting to partner will take a while but with hard work you will get there!
How do I get there?
New day, new stream
Stream for 25 hours in the last 30 days. You might want to start freeing up your schedule a bit.
Like clockwork
Stream for 12 unique days in the last 30 days.
Who watches the watchers?
Reach 75 average viewers in the last 30 days. This is the one that takes a while to complete, just keep your viewers coming back and bringing friends by being your own unique you!
